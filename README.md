## About
Exploratory analysis of retail store sales covering revenue trends, 
channel performance, product profitability, geographic reach, 
and seasonal demand patterns. Includes ABC classification to 
prioritize inventory and business decisions.

## Tools
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, abc_analysis

## Dataset
Educational dataset (retail sales, multi-year, Europe & Asia)

## Key Findings

**Revenue**
- Peak revenue of $285M reached in 2012; declined 28.7% year-over-year
- Highest-revenue categories: Office Supplies, Household, Cosmetics
- Most profitable category: Cosmetics
- Least profitable: Personal Care, Beverages, Fruits

**Geography**
- Present in 45 countries across Europe and Asia
- Top markets: Czech Republic, Ukraine, Bosnia and Herzegovina, 
  Macedonia, San Marino
- Least profitable market: Monaco

**Seasonality**
- Peak order days: Monday and Friday–Sunday
- Elevated demand for Snacks and Vegetables from March to September
- Most profitable months: May, June, July
- Average order processing + shipping time: 24 days

**Recommendations**
- Revise pricing strategy for own-brand products
- Expand Snacks category sales year-round
- Increase marketing for Office Supplies, Household, and Cosmetics
- Reduce shipping time to improve customer experience
